Reviewer Note

This small utility is included to make the listening example easier to evaluate
without additional hardware setup. It sends webcam-based head-orientation data
to the IEM SceneRotator in real time, allowing a direct comparison between
static binaural reproduction and head-tracked binaural reproduction in the
provided REAPER session.

Software repository:
https://github.com/joambi/SceneRotatorHeadTracker

macOS app download:
https://github.com/joambi/SceneRotatorHeadTracker/releases/download/v0.2-beta/SceneRotatorHeadTracker-macOS-v0.2.dmg

Suggested use:
1. Open the REAPER session.
2. Open `SceneRotatorHeadTracker.app`.
3. Select a camera and press `Start`.
4. Press `Calibrate` while looking straight ahead.
5. Listen over headphones and compare the static and head-tracked conditions.
